WebTech Project Proposal

Tattoo Directory

  • About: My prospective website would be a directory for tattoo art and design.
  • Objective: My objective is to inform users about the many aspects regarding getting a tattoo. I'd like to emphasize the difference between tattoo ideation, artist-search, preparation, care, and aftercare phases.
  • Target Audience: My target audience would be 18-24 gen-z users who are prospective tattoo clients that desire educational tattoo resources or artistic inspiration.
  • Content: Content may include links to local shops (within the Boston area) along with respective ratings, tattoo style explanations (i.e, American Traditional vs. Neo-Traditional vs. Japanese style, etc) as well as links to visuals, popular tattoo templates/ideas, as well as educational resources to the healing process of a tattoo
