As the name suggests, Neotraditional tattoos are the evolution of traditional tattoos. Although physically distinct from American Traditional, Neo Traditional uses the same foundation of techniques to complete tattoos, such as heavy black ink outlining. Neo Traditional tattoos are influenced by art movements such as Ukiyo-e Japanese prints, Art Nouveau, and Art Deco. Neo Traditional tattoos are recognized for their complex, luxurious aesthetic, which frequently depicts flowers, women's portraits, animals, and other subjects. The main difference between traditional tattoos and neo traditional tattoos is that neo traditional tattoos often have a broader color palette and a broader range of motifs. Pieces done in this style are known for their lush, decorative details as well as the use of natural imagery such as florals and animals.
